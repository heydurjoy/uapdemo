After you publish

When a routine is published, it becomes the official reference for students, faculty, and rooms on your scope (program, section, or campus).

If anything changes later—room swap, time shift, section merge, or a new slot—the system tracks which people and resources are affected.

Affected parties are notified automatically: in-app alerts plus email stating that their routine may have changed, with a link to the updated grid and a short diff of what moved (demo—wire your notification service in production).

Typical recipients:

  • Students enrolled in the touched sections
  • Faculty teaching those slots
  • Lab / room coordinators when venues change
